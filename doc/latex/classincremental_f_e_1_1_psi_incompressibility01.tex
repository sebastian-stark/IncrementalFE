class incremental\+F\+E\+::\+Psi\+Incompressibility01$<$ spacedim $>$}

Class defining an incompressibility constraint in that the determinant of a symmetric or unsymmetric rank 2 tensor is constrained to 1.

$h^\Omega_\rho = -p\left( J - 1 \right)$,

where $J=\det(\boldsymbol{Q})$, $\boldsymbol{Q}$ is a rank 2 tensor and $p$ the Lagrangian multiplier (the \char`\"{}pressure\char`\"{}).
