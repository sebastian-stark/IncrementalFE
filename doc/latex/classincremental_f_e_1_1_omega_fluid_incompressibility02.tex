\subsubsection*{template$<$unsigned int spacedim$>$\newline
class incremental\+F\+E\+::\+Omega\+Fluid\+Incompressibility02$<$ spacedim $>$}

Class defining Lagrangian multiplier term for an incompressible fluid.

$ \omega^\Sigma = \dfrac{J}{V^\mathrm{f}_\mathrm{m}}\boldsymbol{F}^{-1} : \left[ \boldsymbol{N} \left( \dot{\boldsymbol{v}} - \dot{\boldsymbol{u}} \right) \right] \eta^\mathrm{f}$,

where $\dot{\boldsymbol{v}}$ is the fluid velocity,~\newline
 $\boldsymbol{F}$ the deformation gradient,~\newline
 $J$ the determinant of the deformation gradient,~\newline
 $V^\mathrm{f}_\mathrm{m}$ the molar volume of the fluid,~\newline
 $\boldsymbol{u}$ the displacement variable,~\newline
 $c^\mathrm{f}$ the fluid concentration,~\newline
 $\eta^\mathrm{f}$ is the fluid potential~\newline
 and $\boldsymbol{N}$ the unit normal vector.~\newline


This is the interfacial counterpart to \mbox{\hyperlink{classincremental_f_e_1_1_omega_fluid_incompressibility01}{Omega\+Fluid\+Incompressibility01}}.

Ordering of quantities in \textbf{ Scalar\+Functional\+::e\+\_\+sigma} \+:~\newline
