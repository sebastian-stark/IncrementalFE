\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$unsigned int spacedim$>$\newline
class incremental\+F\+E\+::\+Omega\+Velocity\+Flux01$<$ spacedim $>$}

Class defining Lagrangian multiplier term coupling fluid velocity to fluid flux.

$ \omega^\Omega = \boldsymbol{t} \cdot \left( \boldsymbol{v} - \dot{\boldsymbol{u}} - \dfrac{V^\mathrm{f}_\mathrm{m}}{J} \boldsymbol{F}\cdot \boldsymbol{I} \right)$,

where $t$ is the Lagrangian multiplier,~\newline
 $\boldsymbol{v}$ the fluid velocity,~\newline
 $\boldsymbol{u}$ the velocity of the moving fictitious skeleton,~\newline
 $V^\mathrm{f}_\mathrm{m}$ the molar volume of the fluid,~\newline
 $J$ the determinant of the deformation gradient,~\newline
 $\boldsymbol{F}$ the deformation gradient related to the motion of the fictitious skeleton,~\newline
 and $\boldsymbol{I}$ is the fluid flux.

\begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
Currently, the derivatives required for the $\alpha$-\/family for temporal discretization are not implemented!
\end{DoxyWarning}
