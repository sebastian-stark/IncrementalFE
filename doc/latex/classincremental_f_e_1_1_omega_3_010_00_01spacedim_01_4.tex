\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$unsigned int spacedim$>$\newline
class incremental\+F\+E\+::\+Omega$<$ 0, spacedim $>$}

Class defining the time discrete approximation of a contribution to the incremental potential depending only unknowns not depending upon time

$ \omega^\mathrm{C} = \omega^\mathrm{C}(\dot{v}, \dot{q}, \mu, q; t) $,

where $\dot{v}$, $\dot{q}$, $\mu$, and $q$ may be vectors.

The time discrete approximation is done either by Miehe\textquotesingle{}s method, by the $\alpha$-\/family, or by the modified $\alpha$-\/family.
