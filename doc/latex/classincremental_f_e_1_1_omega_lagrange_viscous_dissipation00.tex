Class defining a domain related scalar functional with the integrand

$ \omega^\Omega = 2 J \boldsymbol{g}:\boldsymbol{d} $,

where $\boldsymbol{d} = \dfrac{1}{2}\left( \boldsymbol{F}^{-1} \cdot \nabla \boldsymbol{v} + \nabla \boldsymbol{v} \cdot \boldsymbol{F}^{-1} \right)$ is the stretching, $\boldsymbol{g} = \boldsymbol{F}^{-1} \cdot \nabla \boldsymbol{f}$ a corresponding Lagrangian multiplier term, $\boldsymbol{F}$ the deformation gradient, and $J = \det\boldsymbol{F}$.

This enforces the equilibrium applicable to a viscous fluid by means of a Lagrangian multiplier.
