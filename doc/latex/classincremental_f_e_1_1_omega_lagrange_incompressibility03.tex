$ \omega^\Sigma = -J \boldsymbol{F}^{-\top} : \nabla \boldsymbol{f} \dot{\xi} $,

where $\boldsymbol{F}$ is the deformation gradient, $J$ its determinant, $boldsymbol{f}$ a Lagrangian multiplier vector and $\dot{\xi}$ the rate of a scalar field.
