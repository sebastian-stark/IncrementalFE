class incremental\+F\+E\+::\+Omega\+Traction00$<$ spacedim $>$}

$ \omega^\Sigma = -\bar{\boldsymbol{f}}(t) \dot{\boldsymbol{u}} $

where $\bar{\boldsymbol{f}}$ is the prescribed traction, and $\dot{\boldsymbol{u}}$ the corresponding displacement.
