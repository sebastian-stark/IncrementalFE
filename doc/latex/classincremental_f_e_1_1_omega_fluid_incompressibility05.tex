\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$unsigned int spacedim$>$\newline
class incremental\+F\+E\+::\+Omega\+Fluid\+Incompressibility05$<$ spacedim $>$}

Class defining Lagrangian multiplier term for an incompressible fluid.

$ \omega^\Sigma = -\dfrac{J}{V^\mathrm{f}_\mathrm{m}}\boldsymbol{F}^{-1} : \nabla \dot{\boldsymbol{v}} \, \eta $,

where $\dot{\boldsymbol{v}}$ is the fluid velocity,~\newline
 $\boldsymbol{F}$ the deformation gradient,~\newline
 $J$ the determinant of the deformation gradient,~\newline
 $V^\mathrm{f}_\mathrm{m}$ the molar volume of the fluid,~\newline
 and $\eta$ a Lagrange multiplier.~\newline


This enforces incompressibility as an interface condition

Ordering of quantities in \textbf{ Scalar\+Functional\+::e\+\_\+sigma} \+:~\newline
\mbox{[}0\mbox{]} $\dot{v}_{,xx}$~\newline
 \mbox{[}1\mbox{]} $\dot{v}_{,xy}$~\newline
 \mbox{[}2\mbox{]} $\dot{v}_{,xz}$~\newline
 \mbox{[}3\mbox{]} $\dot{v}_{,yx}$~\newline
 \mbox{[}4\mbox{]} $\dot{v}_{,yy}$~\newline
 \mbox{[}5\mbox{]} $\dot{v}_{,yz}$~\newline
 \mbox{[}6\mbox{]} $\dot{v}_{,zx}$~\newline
 \mbox{[}7\mbox{]} $\dot{v}_{,zy}$~\newline
