\subsubsection*{template$<$unsigned int spacedim$>$\newline
class incremental\+F\+E\+::\+Omega\+Dual\+Fluid\+Dissipation05$<$ spacedim $>$}

Class defining Lagrangian multiplier term for equilibrium condition in the case that fluid flows without dissipation, where ions flow in addition with dissipation through the fluid.

$ \omega^\Omega = \left[ c^\mathrm{f} \boldsymbol{F}^{-1} \cdot \left( \dot{\boldsymbol{v}} - \dot{\boldsymbol{u}} \right) \right] \cdot \left(\nabla \eta^\mathrm{f} + \sum^I_{i=1} \dfrac{c^i}{c^\mathrm{f}} \nabla\eta^i \right) - \eta^\mathrm{f} \dot{c}^\mathrm{f}$,

where $\dot{\boldsymbol{v}}$ is the fluid velocity,~\newline
 $\boldsymbol{F}$ the deformation gradient,~\newline
 $\boldsymbol{u}$ the displacement variable,~\newline
 $c^\mathrm{f}$ the fluid concentration,~\newline
 $c^i$ are the ion concentrations ( $i$ runs from $1$ to $I$),~\newline
 $\eta^\mathrm{f}$ is the fluid potential,~\newline
 and $\eta^i$ are the ion potentials

\begin{DoxyWarning}{Warning}
Currently, the derivatives required for the $\alpha$-\/family for temporal discretization are not implemented!
\end{DoxyWarning}
