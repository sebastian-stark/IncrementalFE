\subsubsection*{template$<$unsigned int spacedim$>$\newline
class incremental\+F\+E\+::\+Psi\+Chemical07$<$ spacedim $>$}

Class defining the free energy of mixing between solvent and polymeric backbone for a hydrogel

$h^\Omega_\rho = \dfrac{RT}{V^\mathrm{f}_\mathrm{m}} \left[ (J-n_0)\ln\left( 1-\dfrac{n_0}{J} \right) - \chi n^2_0 \dfrac{1}{J} \right]$,

where $R$ is the gas constant, $T$ the absolute temperature, $V^\mathrm{f}_\mathrm{m}$ the molar volume of solvent, $n_0$ the initial volume fraction of the polymer, $\chi$ a material parameter, and $J = c^\mathrm{f} V^\mathrm{f}_\mathrm{m} + n_0$ the determinant of the deformation gradient expressed in terms of the solvent concentration $c^\mathrm{f}$.
