\subsubsection*{template$<$unsigned int spacedim$>$\newline
class incremental\+F\+E\+::\+Psi\+Neo\+Hooke\+Lagrange00$<$ spacedim $>$}

Class defining a scalar functional enforcing the equilibrium equation associated with a compressible Neo-\/\+Hooke material by means of an Lagrangian multiplier

The integrand is

$h^\Omega_\rho = \boldsymbol{\lambda} : \left( \mu \boldsymbol{F} + \lambda \dfrac{\ln J}{J} - \mu \dfrac{1}{J} \right)$,

where

$J$ the determinant of the deformation gradient $\boldsymbol{F}$, $\boldsymbol{\lambda}$ are the Lagrangian multipliers, and $\mu$ and $\lambda$ Lame\textquotesingle{}s constants.
